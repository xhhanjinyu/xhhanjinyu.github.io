Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at Intelligent Media Analysis Group (IMAG Lab), School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanjing University of Science and Technology (NJUST), under the supervision of Prof. Jinhui Tang. My research interests include image processing and computer vision, with a primary focus on video/image segmentation problems. If you are interested in my research, please feel free to contact me (with any questions, suggestions, or requests for collaboration).
